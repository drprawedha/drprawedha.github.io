Dhantel Rhesa Prawedha is an Indonesian information technology professional and open-source software enthusiast based in Central Java, Indonesia. He is currently serving as Assistant IT Manager and Graphic Designer at Swiss-Belinn Saripetojo Solo.[1] He is also an active supporter of the Linux community through his involvement with Paguyuban Linux Solo.[2]

Prawedha has over a decade of experience in hospitality IT support, network administration, server maintenance, and graphic design. His technical background spans Windows Server, Linux infrastructure, Active Directory, and property management systems used in the hotel industry.

Career

Swiss-Belinn Saripetojo Solo (2024–present)

Since June 2024, Prawedha has served as Assistant IT Manager and Graphic Designer at Swiss-Belinn Saripetojo Solo, a hotel property under the Swiss-Belhotel International group.[1]

The Royal Surakarta Heritage — MGallery by Sofitel (2019–2024)

From December 2019 to June 2024, he held the position of Cluster IT Support at The Royal Surakarta Heritage, a luxury heritage hotel affiliated with the MGallery by Sofitel brand. His responsibilities included maintaining internal office networks, troubleshooting hardware and software issues, managing the Rhapsody PMS, Windows Server and Active Directory, and administering the Onity door lock system.[1]

Novotel & ibis Solo (2018–2024)

Concurrently from December 2018 to June 2024, Prawedha served as IT Support Officer at the Novotel and ibis Solo properties. His role covered administration of Windows Server and Linux Server environments, Active Directory management, and network troubleshooting.[1]

Khusyu Parenting (2022–2023)

He briefly served as Assistant Admin and Graphic Designer at Khusyu Parenting between March 2022 and March 2023, combining administrative support with visual design work.[1]

Freelance network administration (2019)

Between July and December 2019, Prawedha undertook independent contract work in network administration. Notable projects included building a VPS on a bare metal server at STIE AUB Surakarta for the Eprints repository system and web server infrastructure, along with monthly maintenance services.[1]

He also worked as a Graphic Designer at Emine Inspiring Hijab (July 2017 – February 2018), an early role that established his visual design capabilities alongside his technical career.[1]
